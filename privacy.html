Forge

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Forge ("Forge", "we", "us", or "our") builds a brotherhood and accountability app that helps Christian men keep their daily disciplines together. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. By using the Forge app (the "Service"), you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to run the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and sync the Service across your devices.
  • Show your check-ins, streaks, notes, and reactions to the members of your own groups — this is the core purpose of the app.
  • Send the reminders and encouragement notifications you opt into.
  • Process and restore your Forge Pro subscription.
  • Understand usage, fix bugs, and improve features.
  • Protect the Service, enforce our Terms, and comply with the law.

3. How Your Information Is Shared

With your brotherhood. Forge is a group accountability app. The disciplines you check in, your streaks, your notes, and your reactions are visible to the other members of the groups you join. Only share what you're comfortable with your group seeing.

With service providers who process data on our behalf, under contract:

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a business transfer.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and associated personal data at any time from within the app (Profile → Settings) or by emailing us at support@famlok.online. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data from our active systems; some content you shared into a group may remain visible to that group in de-identified form, and we may retain limited records as required by law.

5. Security

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS, and access to your data is protected by per-user database security rules. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard safeguards.

6. Children's Privacy

Forge is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Choices

  • Notifications: turn reminders on or off in the app or in your device's Settings.
  • Profile: edit your name, disciplines, reminder time, and language at any time.
  • Access & deletion: request a copy of your data or delete your account as described above.
  • Analytics: contact us to opt out of product analytics.

8. International Users

Forge is operated from, and stores data in, the United States. If you use the Service from outside the U.S., you understand your information will be processed in the U.S.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.
